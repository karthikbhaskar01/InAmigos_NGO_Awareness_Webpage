Who We Are

Building a Compassionate, Inclusive Society

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla. Licensed by the Central Government under Section 8 of the Companies Act 2013, we operate with full transparency and accountability across multiple Indian states.

Our mission is to unite driven individuals to create lasting, positive change — empowering communities, promoting sustainable development, and driving impactful transformation.

We are proud to be registered with NITI Aayog, hold 80G & 12A certifications for donor tax benefits, are CSR-1 registered for corporate partnerships, and carry the IAF ISO 9001:2015 quality certification.

Our Vision

To create a world where every individual — regardless of background — has the opportunity to grow with dignity, purpose, and hope. We believe small, consistent acts of kindness can transform entire communities.

Our Initiatives

Six Flagship Projects, One Mission

Each project addresses a specific, urgent need — together they form a holistic approach to social development across India.

🍱

Project Seva

Food and clothing distribution to underprivileged communities, daily wage workers, and the homeless. Ensuring no one sleeps hungry or suffers in extreme weather conditions.

50,000+ meals served
📚

Project Bachpanshala

Quality education for underprivileged children in rural areas — providing learning sessions, school supplies, and mentorship to break the cycle of poverty through literacy.

Rural children educated
🌱

Project Prakriti

Environmental sustainability through tree-planting drives, clean-up campaigns, and conservation workshops — building awareness for a healthier planet for future generations.

20,000+ trees planted
🦅

Project Udaan

Women's empowerment through digital literacy, vocational training, and skill-building. Helping women find financial independence, start microenterprises, and become community leaders.

900+ women empowered
🐾

Project Jeev

Animal welfare and protection — feeding stray animals, advocating against abuse, and nurturing a culture of compassion and coexistence in our communities.

50+ animals fed daily
⚙️

Project Vikas

Youth skill development and internship training — equipping thousands of young Indians with digital, professional, and leadership skills to thrive in the modern world.

Thousands trained nationally

Why This Work Matters

Breaking the cycle of poverty

Education through Bachpanshala and skill-building via Vikas give children and youth the tools to build independent, dignified futures.

Food security for vulnerable communities

Project Seva ensures that daily wage workers, homeless individuals, and struggling families receive nutritious meals and essential clothing.

Women's economic independence

Through Project Udaan, women across 3 states have started over 250 microenterprises, with 100+ women now training others in turn.

A greener, healthier India

Prakriti's tree-planting and clean-up drives foster environmental responsibility and combat climate change at the grassroots level.

🌏 Aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goals

  • SDG 1 — No Poverty (Project Seva, Udaan)
  • SDG 2 — Zero Hunger (Project Seva)
  • SDG 4 — Quality Education (Bachpanshala)
  • SDG 5 — Gender Equality (Project Udaan)
  • SDG 13 — Climate Action (Project Prakriti)
  • SDG 15 — Life on Land (Project Jeev, Prakriti)
